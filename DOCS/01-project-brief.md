# 01 — Project Brief

## What this app is

Health-First is an AI-powered digital health coaching mobile app built for urban Indian professionals who have tried and failed with health apps before.

The core insight: most health apps fail not because users are lazy, but because the apps punish gaps with streak resets, shame users back into engagement, and ignore the reality of busy professional lives. Health-First takes the opposite approach.

## Who it's for

Urban Indian professional, 25–40 years old. Works long hours, travels, has inconsistent routines. Has tried at least one fitness or health app before and stopped using it. Main barriers: motivation loss after missing a few days, overwhelming targets, impersonal advice.

## Why it exists

To be the app that doesn't make you feel bad for being human. Every design and AI decision in this app is made in service of one principle: **meet users where they are, not where we wish they were**.

## Core philosophy

- No streak counters (ever)
- When a user goes quiet, reach out warmly — don't wait for them to feel guilty enough to return
- When a user returns after a gap, give them an easier plan — not the same plan with a reset
- AI messages should feel like a knowledgeable friend, not a trainer or a notification
- Progress is measured in days logged this week (Mon–Sun), not cumulative streaks

## Five core screens

1. **Onboarding** — captures past-failure context, goal, schedule
2. **Home** — 3-tap daily check-in + AI coach message
3. **Log** — food + sleep + stress in one screen
4. **Coach** — AI message thread with tappable responses
5. **Progress** — weekly insight card + days logged this week

## Two background behaviours

1. **48-hour silence detector** — checks for inactive users every 6 hours, sends a warm check-in message
2. **Comeback plan** — when a user returns after 5+ days, generates a lighter re-entry plan automatically
